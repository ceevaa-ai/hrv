Autonomic Nervous System Interpretation
HRV reflects the balance between sympathetic (fight-or-flight) and parasympathetic (rest-and-digest) activity. Your 2025 peak — particularly Jan–Feb at 50–54ms — indicates strong parasympathetic dominance and good vagal tone. The 2026 data suggests chronic sympathetic activation (sustained stress response), which suppresses parasympathetic recovery. This is a well-documented physiological response to prolonged financial and professional uncertainty.

The magnitude (3–4ms) is modest but consistent across all three years, suggesting a habitual pattern rather than isolated events.
